About Careem

Simplifying lives and empowering communities across the Middle East, North Africa, and Pakistan.

Our Story

From a small startup to a regional powerhouse

Building the Future

Founded in 2012, Careem started with a simple mission: to simplify lives. What began as a ride-hailing service has evolved into a comprehensive platform that serves millions across the region. Today, we're part of the Uber family, combining local expertise with global innovation.

We've grown from a small team to over 4,000 employees, serving more than 100 cities across the Middle East, North Africa, and Pakistan. Our commitment to simplifying lives extends beyond transportation - we're building a platform that empowers communities.
