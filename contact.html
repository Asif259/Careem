Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a ride?

Simply open the Careem app, enter your destination, select your ride type, and confirm your booking. A captain will be assigned to pick you up.

How do I track my food order?

Once you place an order, you'll receive real-time updates on your order status. You can track it from restaurant preparation to delivery at your door.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and Careem Pay. You can also save multiple payment methods for faster checkout.

How do I become a partner?

Visit our Partners page to learn more about partnering with Careem. You can apply online and our team will get in touch with you.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use enterprise-grade security measures to protect your personal and payment information. Your privacy is our priority.

What is Careem Plus?

Careem Plus is our subscription service that offers unlimited free deliveries, exclusive discounts, and premium perks across all Careem services.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our top priority

Comprehensive Safety Measures

We implement rigorous safety measures across all our services. All captains undergo background checks and vehicle inspections. We use real-time tracking and have a 24/7 safety response team ready to assist.

For food deliveries, we ensure proper hygiene standards and contactless delivery options. Your safety and well-being are our highest priorities.
